ChinChin

Description

This is a salty and sweet Nigerian snack made using Flour.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • 1 Cup Butter (Room Temp)
  • 2 Tablespoons Milk
  • 1 Cup Vegetable Oil

Steps

  1. Step 1: Mix All Ingredients in a bowl until dough comes together
  2. Step 2: Roll dough 1/4-inch thick on a floured work surface and cut into small pieces.
  3. Step 3: Heat oil in a deep-fryer or large saucepan to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Fry dough pieces in batches until they turn golden brown and float to the surface, 3 to 5 minutes.
  4. Step 4: Serve
