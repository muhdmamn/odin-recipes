Soaking Garri

Description

This is an easy to make cassava flake treat that is quite common in Nigeria.

Ingredients

  • Garri (Raw)
  • Milk (Optional)
  • Sugar
  • Water
  • Groudnut (Optional)

Steps

  1. Step 1: Pour All Ingredients in a bowl or cup
  2. Step 2: Add water and sieve out unwanted residue from the garri.
  3. Step 3: Add water again and mix together
  4. Step 4: Serve
